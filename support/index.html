Support

We aim to answer every message within seven business days, often sooner. If something inside Faro isn't behaving the way you expect, please write — even small reports help us ship a better app.

Contact

Email: hello@khassinx.com

Please include the device model and the iOS / iPadOS / macOS / watchOS version. Tell us what you saw and what you expected. Because your content is end-to-end encrypted, we can't see your conversations — so the more context you can describe, the faster we can help. Never send another person's real data in email.

Common questions

How do I set up a bond with my child?

In the Faro adult app, create a new bond. Faro generates an invitation to enter on the child's device. When pairing, both screens show a short sequence of symbols — read them to each other on a call, confirm they match, and the bond is verified. From then on, the channel is private and sealed end to end.

Is Faro safe for my child?

Faro is a closed channel. Your child can only talk to the adults you've verified — no strangers, no discovery, no friend requests. The AI assistant is kid-safe and on-device, and gently points a child back to you on sensitive topics. See Security for the full picture.

Can the other parent or household read our messages?

No. Each bond is end-to-end encrypted with its own keys. A bond that isn't theirs simply doesn't exist anywhere they can reach. We can't read it either — we hold no keys and store nothing on our servers.

How do I report or block a contact?

Inside Faro, open the contact and choose Report or Block. Blocking stops further messages immediately. A report shares only what you choose to send us — we never read your conversations.

How do I make a call?

Tap the call button on a bond. Calls use Apple FaceTime, which is end-to-end encrypted by Apple. Faro never records the audio or video of a call — it only keeps a simple activity entry (time and duration) for you.

Why does the assistant redirect some questions to me?

By design. On sensitive topics, Faro's assistant gently guides your child back to you rather than answering for you. It's a bridge, not a replacement for a parent. The AI runs entirely on-device and never profiles your child.

Where is my data stored?

On your devices and in your personal iCloud (encrypted by Apple, your Apple ID). Faro keeps nothing on any server we control. See Privacy for full detail.

How do I access, export, or delete my data?

Everything is visible in the app at all times. In Settings you can export your data, and you can delete your account and content in one place. Because we hold no copy on a server, deletion is complete once your content is removed from your device and your iCloud.

Do the safety and emergency features cost anything?

No. All safety and emergency features are free and never behind a paywall. Optional paid features (a one-time unlock and an optional subscription) only add convenience and personalization — never the things that keep your child safe.
