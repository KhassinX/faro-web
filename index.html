Stay close.
No matter the distance.

Faro is the private, secure place where you and your child stay close across any distance — with no strangers, no ads, and no one else in the room.

Coming to the App Store · Built natively for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch

Faro is built by KhassinX, an independent studio for privacy-first Apple platform apps.

Not another messaging app

Faro isn't built for adults talking to adults. It's built for the one relationship that needs a clean, private, gentle channel of its own: a parent or trusted adult and a child who live apart. A quiet bridge where closeness can grow — without anyone else able to read it or interfere.

Three things Faro protects

A true sanctuary

A walled garden with no strangers, ever. Only adults you've verified — one by one, face to face on a call — can reach your child. The AI assistant is built to be gentle and kid-safe: when a hard topic comes up, it points your child back to you instead of answering for you.

Real connection

A shared canvas you draw on together, time capsules sent to the future, a growing garden of shared memories, and one-tap calls. The small, warm rituals that keep a bond alive when you can't be in the same room.

Peace of mind for you

A calm hub for every bond you hold, gentle weekly insights written on your device, and quiet suggestions of what to ask — never leading, never invasive. Your child never gets caught in the middle of grown-up conflict.

Privacy you don't have to read the fine print to trust

Data Not Collected

Faro's App Store privacy label is the strongest one Apple offers: Data Not Collected. We don't gather your messages, your child's activity, or a profile of either of you. There's nothing to sell, because there's nothing collected.

Sealed end to end

Every message, drawing, and memory is end-to-end encrypted. They're sealed on your device and only open on the other side of the bond. The other household can't read them. Neither can we.

Nothing on our servers

Faro has no server collecting your family's life. Your content lives on your devices and in your own iCloud, controlled by your Apple Account — not by us. We never see it, and we have no copy.

On-device intelligence

Faro's AI runs entirely on your device with Apple Intelligence. Your conversations are never sent off your iPhone, iPad, or Mac for any AI to process — not to us, not to anyone.

No ads. No tracking.

No advertising. No third-party analytics. No tracking SDKs. No data brokers. Faro is for two people who love each other — never for anyone trying to reach them.

You're in control

A grown-up sets up every bond and consents on the child's behalf. You can report or block from inside the app, and access, export, or delete your data whenever you choose.
